In the coming hours, the first advertisements of public channels and groups that have activated the FreePromo plan will appear in a non-invasive manner at the top of search results.
FreePromo is the promotional service that can be activated by anyone via the
@Lyzembot
bot. Free of charge, it can be renewed in this first stage without any restrictions.
It represents a world-first showcase for promoting both one's own public channels/groups and those of others, regardless of whether one is an owner/administrator or a simple user.
A relevant aspect to attract users to one's own channels/groups, in addition to promotions, is indexing of published content. Doing so will help direct them to the respective channel/group. To automatically index any post longer than 250 characters, simply add
@Lyzembot
to your public channels/groups.
It should be noted that
@Lyzembot
is replacing IndexPost, which will be discontinued by the end of this year.
