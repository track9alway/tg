BLC: available from April 27, 2023
BanklessCoin (BLC) - anonymous, untraceable and not trackable like the physical cash - opens to the public on 27 April 2023. It is conveniently managed by a web application that, for added privacy, is only available through the Tor network.
BLCs are purchased in Bitcoin directly from the web app, or with one's own currency via voluntary supporters. Purchasing via volunteer supporters, or using their code, gives a reward of 10% of the purchased BLC value.
Anyone can become a voluntary supporter by divulging the relative code in the "
Earning Opportunities
" section of the web app.
BLC is not subject to fluctuation - its value remains constant over time. 1 BLC is worth 27.9432 USD.
